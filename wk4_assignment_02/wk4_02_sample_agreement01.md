**COMPREHENSIVE RESIDENTIAL RENTAL AGREEMENT**

This Residential Rental Agreement (the “Agreement”) is made and entered into on **April 21, 2026**, by and between:

**Landlord**:  
**Elena Rodriguez**  
Address: 1234 Maple Grove Lane, Suite 200, Seattle, WA 98101  
Phone: (206) 555-0198  
Email: elena.rodriguez@mgproperties.com  

**Tenant(s)**:  
**James Thompson** and **Lisa Chen**  
Current Address: 5678 Birch Road, Seattle, WA 98102  
Phone: (206) 555-7845  
Email: james.thompson@email.com  

**Premises**:  
Landlord leases to Tenant the residential property located at:  
**890 Elm Street, Unit 3C, Seattle, WA 98105**  
(hereinafter referred to as the “Premises”), consisting of a 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment of approximately 1,150 square feet, together with one assigned covered parking space (#42) and a storage locker (#17).

**1. Term**  
This is a fixed-term lease commencing on **June 1, 2026** and ending on **May 31, 2027** (the “Lease Term”). Upon expiration, the tenancy shall automatically convert to a month-to-month tenancy unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **60 days** prior to the end of the Lease Term.

**2. Rent**  
Tenant agrees to pay monthly Base Rent of **$2,650.00**, due on or before the **1st** day of each month.  
- Prorated first month rent (if applicable): $1,767.00  
- Late charge: $100.00 if not received by the 5th, plus $15.00 per additional day.  
- Acceptable payment methods: Electronic funds transfer via Tenant Portal, Zelle, or cashier’s check.  
- Returned payment fee: $50.00 per occurrence.

**3. Security Deposit and Fees**  
Tenant has paid a Security Deposit of **$2,650.00**.  
Additional non-refundable fees:  
- Pet fee: $500.00 (one-time)  
- Cleaning fee (upon move-out if needed): up to $350.00  
- Key/rekey fee: $150.00 if keys are not returned.

The Security Deposit will be held in accordance with Washington state law and returned (less lawful deductions) within **21 days** after Tenant vacates and provides a forwarding address.

**4. Utilities and Services**  
Tenant shall be solely responsible for and pay directly: Electricity, Gas, Water/Sewer, Trash/Recycling, Internet/Cable, and Telephone.  
Landlord shall provide: Structural maintenance, common area lighting, and building insurance on the structure only.

**5. Use of Premises**  
The Premises shall be used solely as a private residence for no more than **four (4)** occupants (the named Tenants and up to two children). No commercial or business activity is permitted without Landlord’s prior written consent.

**6. Pets**  
One (1) dog weighing less than 40 lbs is permitted with prior written approval and payment of the pet fee and monthly pet rent of **$50.00**. Tenant is fully responsible for all pet-related damages, odors, and disturbances. Aggressive breeds are prohibited.

**7. Maintenance and Repairs**  
Tenant shall keep the Premises in clean and sanitary condition and promptly notify Landlord of any needed repairs. Tenant is responsible for damage caused by negligence, misuse, or guests. Landlord shall maintain the Premises in habitable condition as required by law, except for damages caused by Tenant.

**8. Alterations and Improvements**  
No alterations, painting, wallpapering, installation of shelves, or fixtures may be made without Landlord’s prior written consent. Any approved improvements become the property of Landlord upon termination unless otherwise agreed in writing.

**9. Right of Entry**  
Landlord or Landlord’s agents may enter the Premises upon **24 hours’** written notice for inspection, repairs, or showing to prospective tenants/buyers. Entry without notice is permitted in case of emergency.

**10. Insurance**  
Tenant shall maintain, at Tenant’s sole expense, a renter’s insurance policy with minimum liability coverage of **$300,000** and personal property coverage of **$50,000**. Tenant’s insurance must name Landlord as an additional insured. Landlord is not responsible for loss or damage to Tenant’s personal property.

**11. Assignment and Subletting**  
Tenant shall not assign this Agreement or sublet the Premises (in whole or in part) without Landlord’s prior written consent, which may be withheld in Landlord’s sole discretion. Any unauthorized subletting or assignment is a material breach.

**12. Rules and Regulations**  
Tenant agrees to abide by the attached **Rules and Regulations** (Addendum A) and any future reasonable rules provided in writing.

**13. Indemnification**  
Tenant agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Landlord from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from Tenant’s use of the Premises or acts/omissions of Tenant or Tenant’s guests.

**14. Default and Remedies**  
If Tenant fails to pay rent or breaches any term, Landlord may terminate the tenancy and pursue all remedies available under Washington law, including eviction, recovery of unpaid rent, and collection of attorney’s fees and costs.

**15. Early Termination**  
If Tenant terminates early, Tenant remains liable for rent until the end of the Lease Term or until a replacement tenant is found (Landlord will make reasonable efforts to re-rent). A re-rental fee of **$500.00** may apply.

**16. Force Majeure**  
Neither party shall be liable for delays or failure to perform due to causes beyond reasonable control (fire, flood, earthquake, pandemic, government order, etc.), except for payment of rent.

**17. Attorney’s Fees**  
In any legal action arising from this Agreement, the prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney’s fees and court costs.

**18. Governing Law**  
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of **Washington**. Venue shall be in King County Superior Court.

**19. Entire Agreement and Severability**  
This Agreement, together with any attached addendums, constitutes the entire understanding between the parties. If any provision is held invalid, the remainder shall remain in effect.

**20. Additional Addendums Incorporated**  
- Addendum A: Rules and Regulations  
- Addendum B: Move-In/Move-Out Inspection Checklist  
- Addendum C: Lead-Based Paint Disclosure (if applicable)  
- Addendum D: Pet Agreement
